The edible fruits of plantain bananas has more starch than the common dessert banana and is not eaten raw.     African garden eggs are a type of eggplant used as a food crop in several countries across Africa. These small, white fruits have a teardrop or roundish shape and are valued for there bitterness. Garden egg is indigenous to sub-Saharan Africa and csn be stored for up to three months by allowing it to dry, which particularly useful in tropical regions where refrigeration may be limited. In Ghanan, garden egg is one of the three most consumed vegetables alongside tomatoes and pepper. It is used in stews and can also be eaten raw. Interestingly, the stems and leaves of garden egge are consumed in some African Countries, although not in Ghana. Due to its spongy texture, garden eggs is sometimes used as a less expensive meat substitute, as it absorbs other flavors well.
